About Us

Together, let’s reduce food waste and feed those in need


Our Mission


At Waste NOT, our mission is to reduce food wastage and support those who go hungry. We connect restaurants, cafés, individuals with excess food to NGOs and the underprivileged who need it.

What We Do

  • Provide an easy way for donors to list leftover or surplus food
  • Allow receivers to browse and claim food by location or type
  • Encourage sustainable consumption and community kindness

Who We Help

Our platform supports non-profits, shelters, orphanages, and low-income families. Every meal shared brings us closer to a better world.

Join Us

Your participation can bring change. Whether you're donating or receiving, you're part of a movement to end food waste and hunger. Let’s feed hope, not landfills.
